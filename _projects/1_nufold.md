## NuFold Multimer (Jan 2026 – Present)

**Role**: Project Leader

NuFold Multimer is a cutting-edge deep learning framework for predicting RNA-RNA complex structures. Building on advances in protein structure prediction, this project extends similar approaches to the challenging domain of RNA complex modeling.

### Key Contributions

- Developing novel neural network architectures for RNA-RNA interaction prediction
- Integrating structural constraints from experimental data
- Creating benchmarks for evaluating RNA complex prediction methods

### Technologies

- PyTorch
- Transformers architecture
- Structure prediction neural networks

*Project ongoing at [Kihara Lab](https://kiharalab.org/), Purdue University*
